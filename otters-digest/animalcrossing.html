Animal Crossing: New Horizens

Animal Crossing is a beloved franchise developed and produced by Nintendo. Its earliest title dates back to 2001 titled Animal Crossing. On March 20, 2020, Animal Crossing: New Horizons was released for the Nintendo Switch. This long awaited title generated excitement from both new and old fans alike and could not have been released at a better time. The core gameplay of Animal Crossing is building up a town from the ground up while befriending animal villagers along the way. Other activities include decorating your home, fishing, digging up fossils and so much more. It is typically seen as a very relaxing experience. This title was focused on building up a town in an island getaway setting. With Covid and the lockdowns taking place at this time, Animal Crossing was the perfect escape from the reality that we lived in. The game has tons of replay value and is constantly being updated with free content. The title won the award for Game of the Year at the Japan Game Awards and was also nominated for Game of the Year at the Game Awards 2020. Anyone with a Nintendo Switch should consider playing this game. It's a safe way to feel as if one is going on a vacation during these difficult times.

-Michael Owen
