The Borderlands Series Iconic Art Direction

To many, video games are seen as a form of art. The franchise, Borderlands, takes this idea to a whole new level. Every game in this franchise has aged well in terms of its graphic presence. This is mostly due to the very unique design used in the games, which is called Cell Shading. This cell shading effect makes the games resemble a comic book. Hard outlines can be found on all of the character models, making small details and colors stand out. The most recent title, Borderlands 3 came out in 2019, but it is still receiving DLC support to this very day. The Borderlands franchise truly is timeless, making it a working example on how much strong and unique art direction can make something iconic.

-Michael Owen
