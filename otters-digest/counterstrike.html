Astralis: Competitive Counter-Strike:Global Offensive Giant

When it comes to competitive CS:GO (Counter-Strike Global Offensive) no other team is as dominant as the Danish powerhouse Astralis. The roster has shown no signs of slowing down this year even with the format of all tournaments switching to the online format. The team consisting of coach Danny Sørensen (zonic), all time great sniper Nicolai Hvilshøj Reedtz (Dev1ce), rifler Peter Rothmann Rasmussen (dupreeh), Mr.Clutch himself Andreas Højsleth (Xyp9x), rifler Emil Hoffmann Reif (Magisk), super substitute Lucas Anderson (Bubzkji) and the greatest in game leader of all time Lukas Eghold Rossander (gla1ve) have been on a tear since late 2018 and are continuing their form of greatness into this new era of online Counter-Strike. They have remained the #1 or #2 ranked team in the world for a staggering 112 weeks in a row as of December 2020 and currently they boast a 78% win ratio in all matches over the last 3 months. The teams signature by the books smart and calculated playstyle as well as their individual talent and excellent leadership are without a doubt the reason for this kind of continued success. The star studded team secured another tournament win over the last week with their 3-1 victory over mousesports in the finals of Dreamhack Masters Winter 2020. Just today (12/11/2020) the team has secured another Grand Finals spot in the Blast Premier Fall Series tournament set to conclude this weekend. Astralis as an organization currently has the record for the most major tournament wins of all time and as the past 3 months have shown us all that they have no plans to stop their display of excellence anytime soon. Will this weekend be another blowout at the Blast Premier Fall finals or will the russian/ukrainian giants Natus Vincere be prepared enough to take them down? If I was asked to place a bet on this insane match coming up, my money would be on the danes.

-Nick Rodarte
