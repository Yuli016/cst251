The History of Destiny

Destiny is a unique series produced by Bungie. Bungie is known for creating popular tiles, such as Halo 3. After developing Halo Reach, Bungie stepped away from the franchise to work on a new series, known as Destiny. Destiny 1 was surrounded by a lot of excitement due to its unique gameplay. It resembled the combat of Halo, making it a space themed shooter. What set it apart from Halo were all of the RPG elements. Everyone gets to choose from 3 classes, being a Warlock, Titan and Hunter. The classes all have unique abilities and skill sets, like grenades and super abilities. Although one of a kind, Destiny 1 launched with many bugs and a lack of content to keep its long term players engaged. Fast forwarding to 2020, Bungie is currently updating and providing DLC for Destiny 2. Destiny 2 has improved in many ways since it came out. For example, crossplay now exists for the game, so those who play it on console and PC can now play together. Due to this, the player base is larger and better than ever. The history of Destiny is an interesting one and I would highly recommend trying it out. The base game is also free to download, so it is definitely with a shot.

-Michael Owen
