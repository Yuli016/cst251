The Gonz: Skateboarder, Artists, Unafraid to Be Himself

Named one of the most influential skateboarders of all time by Transworld Skateboard Magazine in 2012, Mark Gonzales “The Gonz” has been creating art for longer than he has been skating. His favorite mediums are paintings and clay sculptures and his style is something to recognize. His bright yet soft colors, simplistic faces and figures have made their way onto skateboard graphics, shoes, apparel, storefronts and gallery exhibitions. Gonz lives his life in ways most are too scared to. His carefree and almost childlike attitude is extraordinary in the way it allows him to not be afraid to fail and not be afraid to do or explore whatever interests him. He has been a part of Adidas skateboarding for nearly 10 years and his art makes its way to their products. Gonz is an inspiration to skateboarders and artists alike in the way he is simply not afraid to be himself, it's something we can all derive inspiration from. Be unequivocally yourself and in doing so you will be happy to find the things you will be able to do, experience or create.

-Nick Rodarte
