Hannibal

Hannibal is a psychological horror thriller television series developed for NBC. It is based on Thomas Harris' novels Red Dragon, Hannibal, and Hannibal Rising. The series focuses on the relationship between FBI special investigator Will Graham and forensic psychiatrist Dr. Hannibal Lecter. Dr. Lecter, a cannibalistic serial killer, works to manipulate the FBI from within.

Hannibal is a show that I first began watching as it aired in 2013. Although it is a short series, 3 seasons (39 episodes), my attention span was much shorter back then. I revisited the series in early November. I was hooked. I watched the entire series in less than a business week. Fans of American Horror Story should definitely watch. It has numerous gory scenes that will have you avoiding meat for weeks.

-Vanessa Garcia
