Content

Otter's Digest is a website dedicated to sharing some interesting media pieces to explore and learn about derived from our peers and our own interests as CSUMB students. Here you'll find the latest in art, music, movies/shows or video games including reviews, descriptive articles, revisitations and explorations of interesting content that is relevant. The team consisting of Yulitsa Pantoja, Vanessa Garcia ,Michael Owen, Ross Maeder and Nicholas Rodarte has brought together pieces of media of interest to share and explore with you on our site and we all hope you find something new that interests you during your stay on our magazine pages. This is the Otter's Digest.
