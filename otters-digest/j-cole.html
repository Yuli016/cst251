J. Cole: Back and Better

Jermaine Lamarr Cole, better known as J. Cole is a rapper, singer and songwriter, who owns and produces his own record label, Dreamville Records. Back in 2014, J. Cole dropped an album called 2014 Forest Hills Drive, which could be considered in the top 10 best albums in the rap industry ever. J. Cole has always been amongst the best of the best, never really making a “bad” song. His writing style and flow is a lot different from others, which is overlooked sometimes by the media and listeners. His messages in his songs are very deep and prevalent in today’s society. His songs have not been on top lately, considering Billboard's Top Charts. But he is back in 2019 writing the album, MIDDLE CHILD, which won BET Hip Hop Award for Best Impact Track. And recently he released “The Falloff” which is very lyrically spoken and impressive. Not to mention, he dropped a new single “Fire Squad” that is in anniversary to his 2014 album. J. Cole may not always have a top song, but the message he conveys and speaks will always top today’s artists.

-Ross Maeder
