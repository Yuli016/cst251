Jack Harlow: Definitely Not a One-Hit-Wonder

Jack Harlow's debut studio album “That's What They All Say” hits the shelves of record stores this week and boasts his class distinguishable sound and impressive feature list. Jackman Thomas Harlow is a Louisville native and up and coming rapper from the south who is out to prove his place in the hip-hop scene and is hell-bent on making sure the hit song “Whats Poppin” is not the only chart topper he intends on making. His debut album boasts impressive features from people like Chris Brown, Adam Levine, Lil Wayne, Bryson Tiller and more. Most importantly the album has already shown excellent reception from fans and music critics alike. Songs like “Rendezvous” and “Face of My City” are riddled with clever bars combined with an incredibly confident delivery cadence. After first listen it was clear to see that this album is bound to make a splash and solidify him as a rapper with potential for the future. He has single handedly put kentucky rap on the nations radar over the last year and this album speaks as an example to why. He is digestible for even the most casual of rap fans and this album is no exception, without a doubt this young man has a promising future in the industry and this album is an exceptional landmark for his newly found fame and success.

-Ross Maeder
