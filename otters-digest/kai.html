Kai - Kai The First Mini Album

KAI is Kai’s solo debut album. This album was first released on November 30th. It consists of 6 songs that highlight how versatile Kai is. The mini album is R&B based. The title track “Mmmh” is described as “a charming straightforward love song that captures his confident attitude.” Mmmh is a minimal track with a simple yet addictive pop melody.

Fans such as myself were pleasantly surprised when we heard that the album was finally going to be released. We had been waiting for it since July when it was first confirmed that Kai was working on a solo project. Although I love every song on this album, my favorite is ‘Ride Or Die.’ The album is available to stream on Spotify and Apple music.

-Vanessa Garica
